Biography

He received the B.Eng. degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2017.
He is now a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yuchong Hu.
His research mainly focuses on computer architecture and cloud storage including erasure coding, in-memory key-value stores, and data deduplication etc.
